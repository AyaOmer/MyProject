About Us

Established back in 2007 under the name "Robocon" by a group of undergraduate students to create a generation capable of competing worldwide in the robotics field and reduce the gap between education and labor market. In addition to helping students get more familiar with electrical and........ mechanical components and share information and resources to develop their technical and soft skills. And by 2014 we expanded our activities and changed the name to what you know today 'Assiut Robotics'. Our vision is to aware all students whether in or out of the team about the importance of robotics fields through organising robotics exhibitions on campus and organising trips to big annual exhibitions like EED (Egyptian Engineering Day). We are annually participating in many international competitions as mate ROV (Remotely Operated Vehicle) and Minesweeper competition in which we scored 1st place internationally in 2019. Since we started, our team made an impact on more than 700 students and has it's unforgettable good reputation in Assiut University and Upper Egypt with our long lasting slogan "Learn How to Learn".
